Privacy Policy

Effective June 2026

Care Crafter Home Care, LLC ("Care Crafter," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our employees, clients, and users. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information — name, phone number, email address, and mailing address
  • Employment information — employee ID, work schedule, and visit records
  • Communications — records of SMS messages sent and received for operational purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely for:

  • Sending operational SMS notifications to Direct Care Workers (timesheet reminders, schedule alerts, training reminders)
  • Managing employee records and scheduling
  • Complying with Pennsylvania home care licensing and Medicaid waiver requirements
  • Responding to employee inquiries and support requests
We do not sell, rent, share, or use your personal information for marketing purposes. Phone numbers collected for SMS communications are used exclusively for operational staff notifications.

3. SMS Messaging

Phone numbers collected for our SMS staff notification program are:

  • Used only to send operational messages to Care Crafter employees
  • Never shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes
  • Stored securely and used only for the stated purpose
  • Removed from our system upon request or when employment ends

To opt out of SMS messages, reply STOP at any time. See our Terms & Conditions for full SMS program details.

4. Data Security

Care Crafter Home Care takes reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Our internal portal uses secure authentication and encrypted connections (HTTPS).

5. Data Retention

Personal information is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill operational and legal requirements, or as required by Pennsylvania state law and Medicaid regulations. Employee data is securely deleted upon request after employment ends, subject to applicable record-keeping requirements.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our communications:

  • Twilio — SMS message delivery. Subject to Twilio's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — Website hosting. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.

These services are used solely for operational purposes. We do not share personal information with these providers beyond what is necessary to deliver our services.

7. Your Rights

Employees and staff may:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about them
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
