SMS Messaging — Terms & Conditions

Staff Notifications Program

These Terms and Conditions govern the SMS messaging program operated by Care Crafter Home Care, LLC ("Care Crafter," "we," "us," or "our") for communicating with our Direct Care Workers and staff.

1. Program Description

Care Crafter Home Care sends operational SMS text messages to its Direct Care Workers (employees) and staff. Message types include:

  • Missed clock-in or clock-out alerts
  • Timesheet submission reminders
  • Training due-date reminders
  • Schedule and visit reminders
  • General staff notices and announcements
  • Compliance and documentation reminders

2. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies depending on operational needs. Employees may receive up to 5–10 messages per month on average. During high-activity billing periods, frequency may be higher.

3. Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard SMS rates from your mobile carrier apply to all messages received. Care Crafter Home Care does not charge any additional fees for SMS communications.

4. How to Opt Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages from Care Crafter Home Care at any time by replying:

  • STOP — to unsubscribe from all messages immediately
  • QUIT, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, or END — also accepted to opt out

After opting out, you will receive a one-time confirmation message. No further messages will be sent unless you re-enroll or contact us directly.

Note: Opting out of SMS does not affect your employment status or obligations.

6. Consent

Direct Care Workers and staff provide consent to receive SMS messages during the employee onboarding process by providing their mobile phone number on employment paperwork. Consent is not a condition of employment.

7. Supported Carriers

Supported carriers include but are not limited to: AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon Wireless, Sprint, Boost Mobile, MetroPCS, and U.S. Cellular. Carrier support for SMS programs may vary.

8. Privacy

Care Crafter Home Care respects your privacy. Phone numbers and personal information collected for SMS communications are used solely for operational staff communications and are never sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
